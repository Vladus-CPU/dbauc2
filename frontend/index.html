How It Works

1

Submit Orders

Buyers post bids (price + qty), sellers post asks — all active in the orderbook.

2

View Orderbook

See aggregated bids & asks in real time — click a price to autofill the form.

3

Run Clearing

System finds the clearing price (where demand = supply) and computes matched volume.

4

Allocate & Execute

Orders are allocated, trades are recorded and both sides get confirmation.

5

Settlement & History

Update remaining order quantities, store trades, and show trade log / receipts to users.
